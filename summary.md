Vikmac AjiraLink Agency Ltd – Company Overview Document
1. Company Summary

Vikmac AjiraLink Agency Ltd is a Kenyan-based manpower and recruitment agency that provides both local and foreign staffing solutions. The agency specializes in sourcing, training, and deploying workers for households, businesses, and employers abroad. Their operations cover domestic work, skilled and semi-skilled labor, and overseas job placements.

2. Core Services

Vikmac AjiraLink offers the following categories of staff:

a. Domestic and Household Workers

Housekeepers / Cleaners

Nannies / Babysitters

Home Cooks

Gardeners

Drivers

b. Care and Health Support

Caregivers (elderly and special needs)

Home-based patient attendants

Support workers

c. Professional and Skilled Workers

Security guards

Warehouse staff

Hospitality workers

Office assistants

General labourers

3. Foreign Job Placement

The agency also recruits and deploys Kenyan workers to employers in:

UAE

Qatar

Saudi Arabia

Oman

Bahrain

Lebanon

Selected European countries

They handle matching, documentation support, training, and travel processes for eligible candidates.

4. Accreditation & Legitimacy

Vikmac AjiraLink appears in official public lists of licensed recruitment agencies in Kenya.

It is recognized as a Foreign & Local Recruitment agency.

The company’s details, registration, and validity status can be verified through official government portals.

5. Approach to Recruitment

Vikmac AjiraLink follows a structured placement process:

Candidate sourcing across Kenya

Vetting and background checks

Training (basic housekeeping, caregiving skills, cooking, etc.)

Matching candidates to client requirements

Deployment and support

Their matching process considers employer preferences such as:

Skills and experience

Age range

Language abilities

Diet restrictions (for cooks)

Special needs (for caregivers or newborn nannies)

6. Key Advantages

Wide network of workers in different categories

Experience with both local and international placements

Offers full-cycle recruitment: sourcing, vetting, training, deployment

Flexible matching based on employer needs

7. Important Considerations

Before engaging with any recruitment agency — including Vikmac AjiraLink — clients/candidates should ensure:

The license is valid at the time of engagement

All agreements are written (salary, duties, working hours, contract length)

Transparency in service fees

Workers are treated ethically and legally

Overseas opportunities include proper visas, contracts, and protections

8. Summary

Vikmac AjiraLink Agency Ltd is a Kenyan manpower and recruitment company involved in domestic staffing, skilled labor sourcing, and overseas worker placement. They are publicly listed as a registered agency and provide a wide range of staffing categories with an end-to-end recruitment process.
